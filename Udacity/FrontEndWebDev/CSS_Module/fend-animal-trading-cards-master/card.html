Burnese Mountain Dog

Originating in the Swiss Alps, this large breed once served as draft animals.

  • Nickname: Berner
  • Weight: 70-120 lbs
  • Life span: 7-8 years
  • Temperament: Docile

A large, heavy dog with a distinctive tri-colored coat. Bernese work well with other pets and around strangers. They are excellent guardians.
